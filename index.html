X+ Security and Privacy Lab

Welcome to the X+ Security and Privacy (X+) Laboratory! The X+ (pronounced “X pluses”) Lab is dedicated to conducting groundbreaking research aimed at creating advanced methods that integrate security and privacy into emerging systems. Our wide-ranging efforts encompass various fields such as AI/ML, HCI, transportation, speech privacy, LLM, and social considerations. Our primary goal at X+ Lab is to actively shape a future where technology plays a vital role in empowering society while safeguarding safety and privacy. Through collaborative partnerships with industry, government, and academia, we strive to make a tangible impact in the real world. Join us on this exciting journey towards a more secure and privacy-conscious technological landscape.
